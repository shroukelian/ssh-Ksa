Accounting Advisory: The Fundamental Pillar for Corporate Success in the Saudi Market

By: SSH Strategic Team March 10, 2025

In light of the major economic transformations the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is undergoing within the framework of Vision 2030, professional accounting advisory has become a necessity rather than a luxury for any company seeking growth and sustainability. Managing accounts and preparing financial reports in accordance with international and local standards requires specialized expertise that ensures full compliance with the requirements of the Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority (ZATCA) and the Capital Market Authority (CMA).

1. What is Accounting Advisory?

Accounting advisory is a group of specialized services provided by professional entities to companies and institutions with the aim of improving their financial management, including:

  • Preparation of Financial Statements: Income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement.
  • Financial Analysis: Evaluating the company's financial position and providing recommendations for improvement.
  • Tax Compliance: Ensuring adherence to Value Added Tax (VAT) and Zakat laws.
  • Strategic Financial Planning: Developing long-term financial plans to achieve corporate goals.
  • Budgeting: Planning for future expenses and revenues.

SSH Strategic Tip:

Accounting advisory is not just a problem-solving service; it is a strategic investment that provides you with a clear financial vision to make better business decisions.

2. Why does your company need specialized accounting advisory in Saudi Arabia?

A. Complexity of the Saudi Regulatory Environment

Saudi Arabia is witnessing a sophisticated regulatory system that includes: ZATCA requirements for periodic returns, the mandatory "Fatoora" e-invoicing system, International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) adopted in the Saudi market, and Capital Market Authority requirements for listed companies.

B. Avoiding Fines and Violations

Tax violation fines in the Kingdom can reach up to 25% of the tax due, in addition to further legal penalties. A specialized accounting consultant ensures constant compliance and avoids any violations.

C. Improving Financial Performance

Professional accounting advisory helps companies: identify and reduce cost leakages, improve cash flow management, maximize available tax exemptions and incentives, and make investment decisions based on accurate data.

Important Statistic: Studies indicate that companies that enlist specialized accounting consultants save more than 15-20% of their annual operational costs by improving financial efficiency.

3. Accounting Advisory Services Provided by SSH

  • Comprehensive Tax Advisory: Providing specialized consultations in Value Added Tax (VAT), Income Tax, and Zakat, with periodic reviews to ensure compliance and maximum tax efficiency.
  • Preparation & Analysis of Financial Statements: Preparing accurate financial statements according to IFRS standards and analyzing them to provide management with a clear picture of the company's financial position.
  • Strategic Financial Planning: Developing integrated financial plans that support corporate growth and expansion goals, with robust financial projections and data models.
  • Internal Review & Auditing: Conducting periodic reviews of financial records and operations to ensure accuracy and integrity before submitting any official returns.

Golden Tip from SSH:

Do not wait until the end of the fiscal year to review your accounts. Periodic quarterly reviews enable you to detect any financial issues early and correct them at a much lower cost.

4. How to choose your accounting consultant in Saudi Arabia?

When choosing an accounting advisory partner, ensure the availability of: Professional Licensing (accreditation from the Saudi Organization for Chartered and Professional Accountants - SOCPA), deep experience in your sector, a qualified team of specialists, modern accounting technology compatible with the "Fatoora" system, and a guarantee of absolute reliability and confidentiality in handling your financial data.

5. Key Accounting Challenges Facing Companies in Saudi Arabia

A. Adapting to Continuous Regulatory Updates: Tax regulations in the Kingdom undergo constant updates that require continuous effort and specialized knowledge.
B. Accounting Digital Transformation: The mandatory e-invoicing system and the shift toward accounting automation necessitate technical expertise for proper implementation.
C. Expansion and Growth: As companies expand, the complexities of managing diverse operations increase, requiring an integrated accounting system capable of handling this volume.

Important Alert:

Accounting errors are not limited to financial fines; they extend to reputational and legal damages that may threaten business continuity.

6. Importance of Accounting Advisory for Different Business Sizes

  • Startups & Small Businesses: Early consultation builds a solid accounting foundation that facilitates management and avoids costly formation errors.
  • Medium Enterprises: The growth stage requires consultations to improve financial efficiency and manage strategic expansion.
  • Large Corporations: Need integrated accounting advisory covering consolidated reporting, IFRS compliance, and financial risk management.
