Financial Funding Advisory: Your Key to Securing Capital for Business Growth

By: SSH Strategic Team March 28, 2025

Funding is the lifeblood of any project or company. In the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with the expansion of the investment environment and the diversity of available funding tools, identifying the optimal funding source and negotiating the best terms has become a real challenge for entrepreneurs and business owners. This is where specialized financial funding advisory services step in to turn this challenge into an opportunity.

1. What are Financial Funding Advisory Services?

These services aim to assess actual funding needs, identify the most suitable sources, and prepare professional banking files to ensure approval and sustainability.

SSH Tip:

Many companies fail to obtain funding not because of a weak project, but because their funding files are not prepared in a way that convinces financiers of the investment's feasibility.

2. Types of Funding Sources in Saudi Arabia

A. Commercial and Islamic Banking

Saudi banks (SNB, Al Rajhi, Riyad, Inma) offer various products such as operational financing, asset financing, and commercial real estate financing.

B. Venture Capital (VC) and Private Equity

There is a growing presence of venture capital firms in the Kingdom, especially in the tech sector, supported by the Public Investment Fund (PIF) and various innovation support programs.

C. Government Funding Programs

  • Social Development Bank: Soft loans for small projects.
  • Kafalah: The SME Financing Guarantee Program.
  • Monsha'at: The Small and Medium Enterprises General Authority support programs.

Key Statistic: Saudi SMEs received financing exceeding SAR 45 billion in 2023, with expectations for this figure to double by 2030.

3. Steps to Obtain the Right Funding

  1. Identify Needs: Accurately determine the required amount, purpose, and a sustainable repayment schedule.
  2. Prepare the Funding File: This includes a business plan, feasibility study, and audited financial statements.
  3. Connect and Negotiate: Select the compatible entity and negotiate profit rates and grace periods.

4. SSH Services in Funding Advisory

  • Preparing professional funding files according to international standards.
  • Leveraging a wide network of relationships with banks and investment funds.
  • Representing your company’s interests in negotiations to achieve the lowest cost of capital.
  • Managing follow-up procedures until the release of funds.

Important Alert:

Do not enter negotiations without a pre-prepared file; the first impression before financiers is irreplaceable, and a weak file may close doors even for promising projects.

5. Common Mistakes in Funding Requests

Requesting funding that does not align with actual needs, failing to prepare realistic financial forecasts, and relying on a single funding source without comparing options.
