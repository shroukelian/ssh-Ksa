The Business Legal Expert: Your Legal and Financial Protection at Every Step

By: SSH Strategic Team March 20, 2025

In today's business world, which is constantly changing regulatorily and legally in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, the presence of a specialized legal expert alongside the company has become an absolute necessity. Legal and financial errors can cost companies millions of riyals or expose them to legal risks that may threaten the continuity of their business. The legal expert team at SSH combines accounting experience and legal knowledge to provide comprehensive protection for your company's interests.

1. What are Business Legal Expert Services?

Business legal expert services include a wide spectrum of legal and financial support:

  • Drafting and reviewing all types of commercial contracts.
  • Customized legal consultations for investments and major deals.
  • Representing the company in commercial disputes and arbitration.
  • Continuous legal and regulatory compliance with government entities.
  • Tax litigation and legal responses to ZATCA inquiries.
  • Assistance in bankruptcy, insolvency, and restructuring procedures.
  • Intellectual property protection for trademarks and patents.

SSH Strategic Tip:

Legal prevention is always much less expensive than the cure. An hour-by-hour contract review is much cheaper than a legal dispute that lasts for months in court.

2. Key Legal Areas Companies Need in Saudi Arabia

A. Corporate Law and Governance

The new Saudi Companies Law (2023) brought fundamental updates to the rights and duties of partners and shareholders. Compliance with these developments requires periodic legal review of Articles of Association and Bylaws.

B. Labor Law and Human Resources

The Saudi Labor Law regulates the relationship between employers and employees in detail. Violations expose the company to fines and lawsuits; therefore, we focus on drafting compliant employment contracts and adherence to the Wage Protection System (WPS).

C. Commercial Contract Law

Contractual issues are among the most common sources of commercial disputes in Saudi Arabia. A legal expert contributes to:

  • Drafting robust commercial contracts that protect your company's rights.
  • Reviewing incoming contracts before signing to detect potential risks.
  • Adding appropriate protection and penalty clauses.

D. E-Commerce Law

Companies operating in e-commerce need to understand the requirements of the Saudi E-Commerce Law, privacy policies, and customer data protection.

E. Competition and Anti-Monopoly Law

The General Authority for Competition (GAC) imposes strict rules on commercial practices. Violations may lead to massive fines or suspension of business activity.

Legal Warning:

Ignorance of the law is not an excuse before Saudi courts. Prior legal consultation saves you exorbitant costs and the risk of business closure later.

3. Commercial Dispute Management

  • Amicable Settlement and Mediation: Fast solutions that preserve commercial relationships and reduce costs.
  • Commercial Arbitration: Professional representation before the Saudi Center for Commercial Arbitration (SCCA).
  • Litigation: Representing your company before commercial and administrative courts with a specialized team.

4. Legal Consultations for Investments and Deals

A. Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A)

Providing comprehensive legal Due Diligence for target companies, drafting deal contracts, and managing legal closing procedures.

B. Partnership and Joint Venture Agreements

Drafting partnership and joint venture agreements that protect the rights of all parties and regulate dispute resolution mechanisms.

C. Foreign Investment

Guiding foreign investors through Ministry of Investment (MISA) requirements, licensing procedures, and regulatory compliance.

SSH Tip:

In any investment deal, allocate a sufficient budget for a prior legal audit (Due Diligence). Its cost is a small fraction of what you would pay if you discovered a legal problem after completing the deal.

5. Responding to Inquiries and Government Audits

When your company receives any inquiry or notice from ZATCA, the Ministry of Commerce, the Ministry of Human Resources, or any other government entity, SSH provides:

  • Reviewing the inquiry and assessing legal risks.
  • Preparing robust formal responses supported by documentation.
  • Professional representation in hearings.
  • Following up on procedures until the final file closure.

6. Intellectual Property for Trademarks

Registering your trademark and protecting it from infringement and imitation is one of the most important legal investments for any company. SSH helps you in:

  • Registering trademarks with the Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property (SAIP).
  • Periodic renewal of registrations.
  • Legal follow-up on infringement cases.
