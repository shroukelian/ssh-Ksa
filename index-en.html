SSH was established in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to be a trusted strategic partner for companies and institutions. We aim to provide integrated solutions that support business growth and enhance the ability to make strategic decisions based on accurate data and clear vision.

SSH offers a comprehensive range of specialized professional services, including high-level bookkeeping that ends with accurate financial statements reflecting the true status of the entity. This enables management to have a clear vision and make more effective decisions. We also provide internal and external auditing, financial and administrative consulting, and dispute support in cooperation with leading law firms in KSA.

Our services extend to financial fraud investigation, forensic accounting, company liquidation, commercial registration services, and trademark registration. SSH also helps companies eliminate the burden of managing government platforms, handling them entirely to ensure smooth operations without violations or delays.

To support your business in KSA efficiently, SSH contributes to helping clients align their operations with Saudi Vision 2030 by developing financial and administrative systems and ensuring full compliance with government standards.
